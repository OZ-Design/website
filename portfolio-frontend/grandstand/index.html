GrandStand in the News

Neymar involvement in Copa or Olympics still undecided - Luis Enrique

Barcelona boss Luis Enrique says no decision has yet been made on whether Neymar will play for Brazil at either the Copa America or Olympic Games this summer. Brazil are facing a busy summer, with the Copa America Centenario running from June 3 to June 26 and the Olympic Games' football tournament running from Aug. 3 to Aug. 20.

Guillermo Ochoa's patience rewarded with role as Malaga starter

Mexico international Guillermo Ochoa said he is grateful to finally have a chance to start as goalkeeper for Malaga and added that he feels his patience and hard work have paid off. The World Cup 2014 star finally has his chance to impress in La Liga following the news that regular Malaga goalkeeper Carlos Kameni will be out for six weeks after surgery.

Brazil coach Dunga wants Neymar at Olympics more than Copa America

Brazil coach Dunga would prefer to see Barcelona star Neymar at the Olympics in August than in the Copa America in the United States in June. Dunga and his staff are in Spain this week to talk to Neymar and Barcelona to decide in which tournaments the forward will play. Officials from the Brazilian football federation said a decision will only be made after an agreement is reached among all parties.

Rafael Esquivel extradited to United States in FIFA bribery case

Switzerland's justice ministry says a former Venezuela football federation president, Rafael Esquivel, has been extradited to face bribery charges in the United States in the widening FIFA case. The Swiss office says two American police officers "took charge of him in Zurich and accompanied him on the flight to New York" on Monday. Esquivel should appear in federal court in Brooklyn within 24 hours.
