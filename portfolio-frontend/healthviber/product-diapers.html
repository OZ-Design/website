Jobst for Men Dress 8-15mmHg provide continuous relief from tired aching legs and mild swelling. They are lightweight and feature a ribbed design.

Jobst uses gradient compression technology to help improve circulation and energize your legs especially when sitting or standing for long periods of time. Made from 97% nylon and 3% lycra-spandex in the USA.

Jobst for Men Dress is a high-quality, fashionable sock designed to complement business or business casual apparel. Lightweight construction and extremely soft yarns deliver unparalleled comfort. Knit-in-heel and ample toe area ensure a perfect fit. Lightly ribbed texture provides a stylish accent to complement any wardrobe. Compression in 8-15 mmHg.
