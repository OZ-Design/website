About us

What is HealthViber?

HealthViber is your one stop health and wellness site. Health and wellness screenings are important because not only are they able to detect problems early, but at a point that it will also be less expensive to treat. While a diagnosis from your doctor may require several trips before you get a plan of treatment, HealthViber provides quick results with a comprehensive health plan that you can immediately begin to implement or share with your health care provider. Patients can also access lab results by setting up an account with a user ID and secure password.

With the rising costs of health care, coupled with the threat of lack of adequate healthcare insurance coverage, health screenings provide a much needed stop gap measure to provide preventive care and early diagnosis, which can prevent further illness and even avert death.

While most physicians may give a diagnosis, many do not give a plan of treatment with specific goals aimed at reaching the optimum health of the patient. It is not difficult to prescribe medicines, but to give the level of quality care required to treat the patient takes time and greater involvement.

With HealthViber we want to reduce the gap between patient and healthcare provider. By offering screenings we reach a population that may not routinely have annual check-ups, or may fear high medical costs. By serving the community, places of faith and businesses, HealthViber brings health screening services to the patient.

With the use of technology, the patient doesn’t have to wait until his or her next appointment, but can access HealthViber’s secure website. HealthVIber is your one stop wellness site with a customized health plan right at your fingertips.

Health Screenings/Wellness Programs

Corporate

Many companies provide health screenings as a part of their overall wellness program. HealthViber can assist with creating a company wide wellness agenda, staff the screenings, and via the secure website, provide a detailed plan of action.
Read more

Small Business

Many small businesses that are not able to provide paid health insurance for members of their staff can benefit greatly by offering onsite health screenings and wellness programs. For significantly less money, the staff will feel that employer has an interest in their health and well being. HealthViber can direct them to the resources that can assist uninsured and underinsured populations, by provided information on clinics, practitioners, and pharmaceuticals available at a reduced cost.
Read more

Places of Worship

Similar to what is stated above, HealthViber seeks to take healthcare to the people. Places of worship are trusted institutions in our culture and provide an array of services to the community that seek to address the needs of the whole person. With a HealthViber screening and wellness program, participants will complete a profile that is confidential and as a part of the health plan receive a referral to a community medical clinic, or if desired, the participant’s personal doctor.

HealthViber wants to do more than an impersonal screening, we have a desire to see healthy communities and believe that comes from early detection, preventive medicine, a plan of action, follow-up and a supportive community of health professionals to help each individual reach his or her health goals. In the follow-up meetings the patient will be able to see how their change in diet or exercise has improved their health. HealthViber will capture this data and help chart the progress of the patient.

As with the other wellness programs, HealthViber will train volunteers to screen and establish an onsite point person to provide information for the uninsured, if a physician referral is needed, assistance with getting medications, or free physical exams.
